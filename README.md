## What is Sakai?
Public transportation in the Philippines is complex. Commuters often don't know the exact fare, the travel time, or the best combination of jeepneys, buses, and trains until they are already on the road. 

**Sakai** solves this by acting as a genuine decision-making tool tailored to your daily priorities. You input your origin and destination, and Sakai generates the most viable public transit routes across Metro Manila (NCR) and Luzon. 

What sets Sakai apart is the **Travel Mode Selection**. Because every day is different, Sakai's AI engine adapts your route based on what matters to you right now:

* **Tipid Mode:** Short on cash? Sakai prioritizes the absolute lowest fare, staying strictly within your inputted budget (even if it means walking a little further).
* **Mabilis Mode:** Running late? Sakai finds the fastest possible route within an acceptable price range to get you there on time.
* **Komfort Mode:** Carrying heavy bags or just exhausted? Sakai calculates routes with the fewest transfers and walking segments.
* **Balanced Mode:** The standard commute. The best compromise between cost and travel time.

By integrating fare transparency, budget tracking, and AI-driven routing, Sakai moves from being just a map to being the ultimate commuter's assistant.
